About Us

For years now,KWS has been integrating MUN as a part of its curricular system.
MUN is a crucial part of Kathmandu World School's education, offering a vibrant platform for students to simulate global diplomacy.
It involves thorough research, critical thinking, and persuasive communication as students take on diplomat roles,
discussing global issues and promoting empathy and teamwork.
This immersive experience fosters a deep grasp of international relations and hones skills in public speaking and negotiation,
cultivating future global leaders with informed perspectives.
